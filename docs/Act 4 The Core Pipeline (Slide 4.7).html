Stage 1: Raw Text

Humans provide text as characters and symbols. To us, "Cat" means a feline. To the computer, it's just a sequence of Unicode bytes.

Input: "The cat sat"
✂️

Stage 2: Tokenization

The tokenizer breaks text into discrete units (tokens). Sometimes these are words, often they are sub-word fragments like "un-" and "happy".

The cat sat
🔢

Stage 3: Token IDs

Each unique token is assigned a number (Index) from the model's fixed dictionary. This allows the model to "lookup" data efficiently.

[464, 3797, 3332]
📐

Stage 4: Vectors (Embeddings)

Finally, numbers become coordinates in high-dimensional space. The model now processes geometry, where "cat" is close to "dog".

[0.12, -0.45, 0.88, 0.01, ..., +1532 more]
