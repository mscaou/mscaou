🎯 Season Goals

Let’s focus on 4 main pillars, each with measurable outcomes:

1. Rebuild the Team

Form all committees, define workflows, and strengthen leadership communication.

Key Metrics:

  • ✅ All committees operational by early November
  • ✅ Weekly leadership syncs established

2. Grow & Engage Community

Expand club visibility, attract new students, and foster community engagement.

Key Metrics:

  • 🚀 25,000+ WhatsApp members
  • 🎉 3+ interactive on-campus events

3. Develop Talent

Build strong technical and professional skills through workshops, mentorship, and practice events.

Key Metrics:

  • 💻 8+ workshops across AI, Web, Cyber, and Blockchain
  • 🧠 Mentorship Program launched mid-year

4. Showcase & Collaborate

Strengthen partnerships, celebrate achievements, and create national collaborations.

Key Metrics:

  • 🤝 Collaborations with NU MSC & national chapters
  • 🌟 “Beyond the Code” year-end showcase
